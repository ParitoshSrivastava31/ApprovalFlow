ApprovalFlow is a client approval platform built for agencies, freelancers, designers, video editors, and service businesses that constantly send work to clients and waste time chasing approvals across WhatsApp, email, PDFs, screenshots, and voice notes. The core idea is simple: every deliverable should have a single source of truth. An agency uploads a piece of work, whether it's a social media creative, landing page design, video, report, proposal, or document, and the platform generates a shareable review link. The client opens the link without creating an account, reviews the work on any device, leaves comments if needed, and either approves it or requests changes with a single click. Every action is automatically recorded in a timeline, including views, comments, approvals, revisions, timestamps, and version history, creating a permanent audit trail that eliminates confusion about who approved what and when. In the early version, agencies simply share the review link however they normally communicate, usually through WhatsApp, while future versions integrate directly with the WhatsApp Business API so approval requests can be sent, tracked, and managed without leaving the platform. The long-term vision is not to become another bloated agency management suite competing with dozens of existing tools, but to become the approval layer that sits between businesses and their clients, turning chaotic conversations into structured decisions. What started as "approve this design" can eventually expand into managing feedback, revisions, asset versioning, client sign-offs, content calendars, onboarding, and other client-facing workflows, but the initial wedge remains extremely focused: helping businesses get faster approvals, reduce misunderstandings, and maintain a reliable record of every client decision. The value proposition is not project management, communication, or collaboration itself; it is making approvals frictionless while preserving accountability.
